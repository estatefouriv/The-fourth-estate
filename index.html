About the Journal

The Fourth Estate is a student-run publication dedicated to rigorous, accessible analysis of politics, policy, society, and culture. We believe student voices belong in public debate.

Submit Your Writing

We publish student-written articles every one to two weeks. Pitches and submissions are open to all students. Write for us.

Editorial Standards

All articles are reviewed by our editorial team for accuracy, clarity, and fairness. We correct errors promptly and transparently. Facts over narratives.
